WV Fish Map

WV Fish Map is a free iOS app that shows West Virginia trout and channel catfish stocking events on an interactive map. The app works offline once data has been loaded, and refreshes automatically when online.

Stocking data

Stocking events are sourced from the West Virginia Division of Natural Resources’ published stocking schedules. We are not affiliated with the WVDNR. Always confirm with the WVDNR before planning a trip; conditions and schedules can change.

Reporting an issue

If something isn’t working, a stream is mislabeled, or you have a feature request, please email hello@spice-lick.com. Including your iOS version and a brief description of the issue helps us track it down faster.

Subscriptions

Some features may require a subscription. Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID and renew automatically until canceled. You can view, change, or cancel a subscription at any time in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.

For refunds, use Apple’s Report a Problem page. Refunds are issued at Apple’s discretion under the App Store terms.
