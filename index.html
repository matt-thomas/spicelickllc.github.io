App development

We design and build native iOS applications, both under our own label and for clients. Our first app, currently in development, is West Virginia Fishing Map — an interactive map of West Virginia trout and channel catfish stocking events, sourced from the West Virginia Division of Natural Resources’ published schedules. Preliminary support and privacy information is available at /wv-fish-map/support.html.

Matt Thomas, Founder

Spice Lick LLC was formed in 2026 by Matt Thomas to bring more than fifteen years of independent software work under a single brand. Matt is a senior full-stack engineer who has worked across healthcare technology, AI/ML, nonprofit fundraising, and consumer applications.

Matt is technical cofounder of Doclytics, an AI/ML-powered OCR and data-entry platform, where he built the MVP and continues to lead engineering. He previously served as technical lead at Scanslated, a healthcare communications company, where he led SOC 2 and HIPAA audits and built FHIR/HL7 integrations. Earlier in his career he spent seven years at Jackson River, helping it grow from an early-stage startup into an enterprise SaaS provider serving large nonprofits.

Matt holds an M.S. in Information Science and a B.A. in English from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He is an Eagle Scout, an FAA-rated private pilot, and a former NOLS Wilderness EMT and ACA Level 4 Swiftwater Rescue Instructor. He lives and works on a small farm in Summers County, West Virginia.
